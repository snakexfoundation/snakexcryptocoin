SnakeX Foundation operates under a governance-first model with progressive decentralization.

## Principles
- Transparency
- Utility-first token design
- Conservative economic structure
- Gradual decentralization aligned with ecosystem maturity

## Governance Structure
In the early stages, governance coordination is managed by the founding contributors. Governance mechanisms may evolve over time to include broader community participation as the ecosystem develops.

## Scope
The Foundation does not position Snake X Crypto Coin as an investment product and does not promise financial returns. Governance activities focus on ecosystem sustainability, application integration, and long-term alignment.
